Kimi Portfolio Manager

A second strategy.
Powered by AI.

Running alongside the SPY system, an autonomous AI manages a separate long-term equity portfolio — selecting stocks, sizing positions, and rebalancing as conditions evolve. Every move fires a live signal to members.

How It Works

An advanced AI system reads live market data, portfolio weights, and macroeconomic context, then decides when to enter, exit, or adjust each long-term position. Trades execute directly on Robinhood, verified and timestamped.

Unlike short-term SPY trading, this strategy holds positions for weeks to months. Each stock is assigned a target portfolio weight, and the system rebalances when conviction changes — adding on strength, trimming at targets, or cutting losses when the thesis breaks.

No human intervenes between decision and execution. The signal you see is the trade.
